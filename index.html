HEROES

Barbarian King

The Barbarian King is the first Hero.He is a larger, stronger, more aggressive, more impressive, and more powerful Barbarian.

Archer Queen

Archer Queen is the 2nd hero the game has to offer and She is a single targeting unit with 725 hit points at the base level.

Royal Champion

The Royal Champion is the fourth Hero in the Home Village. She has moderately high DPS, moderate health, and targets defenses.

Clash of Clans is an online multiplayer game in which each player is a village chief. Every player builds their village by using resources and trains troops to attack other player villages to gain resources and trophies.

Clash of Clans has a worldwide fan base due to the simple, yet highly entertaining gameplay features. Millions of players log-in each day to chat with other clan members, upgrade their base, practice new attack strategies, and participate in Clan Wars.
        

Advantages: You learn how to set up strategy. You can learn how to analyze a situation and find the solution accordingly. You learn about timing, what comes first and what comes next.

Many players agree that having most of your base concentrated in the middle is a good strategy. Keep your key buildings in the most defensible position. Place your storages and town hall in the center of your base, behind the defenses and walls. Put builders huts in the corners.
